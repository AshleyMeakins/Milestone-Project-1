Pendulum are back!

In october of 2011, it was announced by band singer rob swire that a new album was in the works, but unfortunately it was later announced that there would be no "new album" for some time. The band didn't fully dissapear though as they still continued to make some appearances at Download Festival, Oxegen, Glastonbury Festival and even opening for Linkin park.

16th September 2020, Driver and Nothing for Free, were released giving us their first new music in 10 years. On September 14th 2020, the band recorded a concert at Spitbank Fort to promote their new music which was streamed live on YouTube for anyone to watch free of charge.
